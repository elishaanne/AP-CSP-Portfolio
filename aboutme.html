All About Me

Elisha Anne Santos

Well this is just a little snippet about me and my life.

Hi, my name is Elisha Anne Santos. I was born on July 1 and I am 16 years old. I am the third Santos sibling out of four kids,
which means I have middle child syndrome a.k.a the forgotten child, but I think I am turning out just fine. I have an older brother,
Ynah, an older sister, Dindi, and a younger brother, Jared. My siblings and I have pretty big gaps as my oldest brother graduated
from Don Lugo in 2011, my sister graduated from Don Lugo in 2018, and my youngest brother is only in 4th grade. Music and
singing are my escape from this fast paced and constantly changing world. I enjoy spending time with my family and close friends,
listening/playing music, playing tennis, eating, sleeping, napping, binge watching on Netflix or Hulu. I am a total sucker for puns
and anything High School Musical related. My favorite thing to do is travel with my family and friends. So far, I have been to Canada,
the Phillippines, Japan, Taiwan, and Mexico. As I grow older, I hope to continue to grow my list of places I have been
and tell the stories of my adventures.
